Safety Message

Pedestrian
Safety

  • Use designated crosswalks and walkways — never cut across active vehicle zones or freight lanes
  • Make eye contact with drivers before stepping in front of any vehicle — assume you are invisible until confirmed
  • Hi-vis vests are mandatory in all dock, ramp, and freight corridor areas — zero exceptions, every shift
  • No phones while walking in vehicle movement zones — distracted walking is a life-safety issue
  • Report every near-miss — our safety culture is built on transparency, not silence
